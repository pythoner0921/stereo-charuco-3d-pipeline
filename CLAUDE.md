### Stereo Camera Constraint
Windows DirectShow gives **exclusive** camera access. OpenCV and FFmpeg cannot access the same camera simultaneously. The SmartRecorder solves this by using OpenCV for both detection and recording (VideoWriter with MJPG fourcc).
